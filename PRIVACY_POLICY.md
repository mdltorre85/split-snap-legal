## 3. How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used only for the following purposes:

- **To provide the App** — authenticate your account, process receipt scans, calculate splits, and display your history.
- **To enforce usage limits** — track your scan count to apply the free tier (5 scans) or confirm your Pro subscription.
- **To communicate with you** — send account-related emails (password reset, etc.). No marketing emails are sent without your explicit consent.

**Your personal information is never sold, rented, or traded to third parties for marketing purposes.**

## 5. Receipt Images

When you scan a receipt:
- The photo is **compressed on your device** before being sent.
- It is transmitted over an encrypted HTTPS connection to the API.
- The image is passed to Anthropic's Claude AI to extract line items.
- **Receipt images are not stored.** They are processed in memory and discarded immediately after parsing.
- Anthropic may retain API inputs per their own data retention policy. See [Anthropic's privacy policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy) for details.

---

## 6. Local Storage

The App stores the following data on your device:

- **Authentication session token** — keeps you logged in between sessions (stored via AsyncStorage on native, browser localStorage on web).

No advertising cookies or tracking pixels are used.

---

## 7. Data Retention

| Data type | Retention period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Account and split history | Until you delete your account |
| Receipt images | Never stored (processed and discarded immediately) |
| Session tokens | Until you sign out or the token expires |
| Scan count | Deleted with your account |

You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen. Upon deletion, all your data is permanently deleted from the database within 30 days.

---

## 8. Data Security

The following measures are in place to protect your data:

- All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption.
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (managed by Supabase Auth).
- Database access is controlled via Row Level Security — your data is only accessible by your own account.
- The Anthropic API key is stored server-side only and is never included in the app bundle.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, you will be notified as required by applicable law.

## 11. EU Residents — GDPR Rights

| Receipt images | AI parsing (not stored) | Performance of a contract |

### 11.2 International Data Transfers

Your data is processed by US-based service providers (Supabase, Anthropic, Vercel, RevenueCat). These transfers outside the EU/EEA are conducted under **Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)** as approved by the European Commission, which provide adequate safeguards for your personal data.

### 11.3 Your GDPR Rights

As an EU/EEA resident you have the following rights:

- **Right of Access (Art. 15)** — Request a copy of the personal data held about you.
- **Right to Rectification (Art. 16)** — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Right to Erasure (Art. 17)** — Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
- **Right to Restriction (Art. 18)** — Request that processing of your data be restricted in certain circumstances.
- **Right to Portability (Art. 20)** — Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
- **Right to Object (Art. 21)** — Object to processing based on legitimate interest.
- **Right to Lodge a Complaint** — You have the right to file a complaint with your national data protection authority (DPA) if you believe your data is being processed unlawfully.

### 11.4 How to Exercise Your GDPR Rights

Email: **splitsnap.privacy@gmail.com**
Subject line: "GDPR Request"

Requests will be responded to within **30 days** as required by GDPR. Identity verification may be required.

### 11.5 Data Retention under GDPR

Personal data is kept only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy. When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days.
