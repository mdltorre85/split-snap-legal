## About SplitSnap

A receipt splitting app powered by AI. Photograph a restaurant receipt and split the bill between friends in seconds.

**Key Features:**
- AI-powered receipt parsing (Anthropic Claude)
- Split up to 6 people
- Assign items per person
- Configurable tip and tax
- Split history saved to the cloud
- Free tier (5 scans) + Pro subscription ($2.99/month)
